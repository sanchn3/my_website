Who I Am

I find satisfaction in uncovering patterns within datasets. My journey into data science began during my mechanical engineering degree, where I discovered the efficiency of problem-solving through programming during computer science courses. This realization ignited my passion for data science. Subsequently, during my master's program, I developed ETL processes and code to analyze COVID hospitalizations spanning from 2019 to 2023. My expertise expanded further when I crafted an application capable of extracting data from APIs and leveraging statistical attributes such as Poisson's ratio and normal distribution to predict outcomes in professional soccer matches. Through Python programming, I aimed to gain a competitive edge against corporate odds setters in the realm of sports betting. Presently, I serve as a data engineer for SDG, engaged in a project collaborating with Roche. This project focuses on building a database in Snowflake using the AWS S3 infrastructure to finally move data to a tableau dashboard. Being bilingual and having a penchant for global exploration, I aspire to secure an international role. In doing so, I aim to not only contribute my expertise as a data scientist but also infuse projects with my diverse experiences and global perspectives.
